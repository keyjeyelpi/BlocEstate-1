Step 2. Fixtures, Chatels and Rentals FIXTURES

Be specific and use full sentences. For Example, instead of saying 'the crystal chandelier', try saying 'the crystal chandelier in the dining room on the first floor'.

CHATELS

Be specific and use full sentences. For Example, instead of saying 'the dining table', try saying 'the black rectangular wooden dining table with its four associated brown velvet chairs located in the dining room on the main floor.'

RENTALS

Be specific and use full sentences. For Example, instead of saying 'the furnace', try saying 'the GE furnace installed 9 September 2016 currently on a 5 year rental term under Energia Heating Company as order number A674 H43 234.'
